# Competitor: HerSolution
_Last updated: 2026-03-14_

## Overview
- **URL:** https://hersolution.com
- **Category:** Direct — women's libido oral supplement
- **Format:** Daily capsule/pill
- **Market:** USA primarily, international
- **Trustpilot:** 4.6★
- **Threat level:** Medium — established brand, loyal customers, but old positioning

## Product
- Daily supplement, 60-day supply recommended for full results
- Claims results in as little as 7 days
- 67-day return policy
- Free shipping on select USA orders

## Positioning & Messaging
- "Doctor-endorsed", "100% safe", "100% natural"
- Headline: "Designed to Dramatically Increase a Woman's Desire for Sex"
- Uses language: "boost", "increase", "dramatically" — old-school framing
- Speaks to: busy women, hormonal changes, stress, poor nutrition
- Tone: clinical, somewhat guilt-inducing ("feeling like less of a woman")

## Their Funnel
- Direct sales website
- Long-form sales page (classic direct response style)
- 60-day supplementation recommendation

## Weaknesses (Bloomin advantages)
- Capsule format — less convenient, less enjoyable than honey sticks
- "Boost/Increase/Dramatically" language — Bloomin's "restore" is more sophisticated
- Guilt-inducing copy ("feeling like less of a woman") — Bloomin empowers
- No cortisol/survival mode mechanism — generic "hormonal imbalance" framing
- Old-school website design
- No Saffron in formula

## Strengths
- Established brand, years of trust
- Doctor endorsement (Dr. Karen Vieira PhD)
- 6+ year loyal customers

## Notes for Monitoring
- Watch for: pricing changes, new bundles, formula updates, new ad angles
