## Brand Mechanism: "Survival Mode" UMS

**The Core Insight:** Chronic stress puts women in Survival Mode. The body suppresses desire because survival takes priority over procreation. This is biology, not a personal failure.

**Why other supplements fail:** They try to BOOST desire while the system is actively suppressing it. _"It's like flooring the gas with the emergency brake on."_

**What Bloomin does differently:** Removes the brake first — so desire can return naturally.

### The 4 UMS Stages:
1. **Diagnosis** — Recognize Survival Mode / cortisol-desire connection
2. **Disqualification** — Why other solutions don't work
3. **Breakthrough** — Bloomin's mechanism: targeting the cortisol-desire blocker
4. **Activation** — Desire returns because the blocker is removed

## Tone & Language Rules

### NEVER use:
- "boost"
- "supercharge"
- "enhance"
- "increase libido"

### ALWAYS use:
- "restore"
- "return"
- "reclaim"
- "make space for"
- "removes what blocks it"
- "brings you back to yourself"

### Voice:
- Warm but direct
- No supplement clichés
- Treat women as intelligent adults
- Science-informed but not clinical
- Speak to the pain before the solution

## Notes for AI Context

- The mechanism (cortisol blocks desire) should be explained simply, not clinically
- Format: honey sticks = daily ritual = effortless consistency
- Always position Bloomin as restoration, not stimulation
- The product is for women who have a good life but lost their spark — not women with medical issues
