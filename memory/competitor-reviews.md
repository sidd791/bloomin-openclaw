## Complaint Themes (Cross-Brand)

### 1. "It didn't work for me" — #1 complaint
Both hersolution and provestra low-star reviews lead with this. Customers felt deceived by marketing promises. No personalization, no explanation of WHY it might not work for their specific situation.

### 2. Results wore off
Provestra: initial effect was good but faded after ~8 weeks. Customers had no framework to understand this — interpreted it as failure. No mechanism explanation provided.

### 3. Timeline mismatch / impatience
"After two weeks no results!" — customers expecting fast results, no expectation-setting in onboarding.

### 4. Shipping & logistics failures
Provestra: lost packages, 15 days delivery, 3 calls to support. No proactive communication.

### 5. Product quality signals
Provestra: bad-smelling pills, no return policy. Cheap product experience erodes trust.

### 6. Counterfeit/scam risk
Hersolution: fake UK replica site. Brand not protecting its identity globally.
