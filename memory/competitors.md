## Direct Competitors (Women's Libido Supplements)

### HerSolution (hersolution.com)
- **Format:** Daily pill/capsule
- **Positioning:** "Doctor-endorsed", "100% safe", "natural"
- **Ingredients:** Niacin, Hops, Ginkgo Biloba, Mucuna Pruriens, Epimedium, Cayenne
- **Angle:** Hormonal + nutritional imbalances causing low libido
- **Language:** Still uses "boost", "dramatically increase desire" — old-school
- **Trustpilot:** 4.6 stars
- **Weaknesses:** Capsule format, old website style, uses "boost" framing
- **Notable:** Has been around a long time, loyal customer base (6yr users mentioned)

### Vigorelle (vigorelle.com)
- **Format:** Topical arousal gel
- **Positioning:** "Instant arousal", doctor-endorsed
- **Ingredients:** L-Arginine, Ginkgo Biloba, Damiana, Wild Yam Root, Vitamins A/C/E
- **Angle:** Blood flow + lubrication focused
- **Different category:** Topical vs oral — less direct competitor but same audience
- **Weaknesses:** Old-school "boost" framing, clinical not warm

### Unfabled (unfabled.co)
- **Format:** Subscription wellness supplements (UK)
- **Positioning:** Sustainable, science-backed women's health
- **Market:** UK primarily
- **Notable:** Featured in Dr. Jolene Brighten's TikTok (360K followers)
- **Strengths:** Strong brand, subscription model, clean positioning
- **Weaknesses:** Broad women's health (not libido-specific)
